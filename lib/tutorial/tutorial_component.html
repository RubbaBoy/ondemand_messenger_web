Sending Messages

Put in the phone number in any format, put in your message and click Send. Google reCaptcha kinda protects you from sending messages in high frequencies.

Address Book

Address books are meant to allow multiple people to track phone numbers and names.

To use an address book, first click Show Address Book and put in a unique book name and a password. Click either Login or Create depending on if you want to access one or create one. You are automatically logged into the book upon reloading, sessions lasting 24 hours.

To add a user to the book, put their name in Name and their number in Number and click Add. Removing a person can be done by clicking the x on the right of their row.

To send a message to a user, click their row and their number with autofill at the top.
